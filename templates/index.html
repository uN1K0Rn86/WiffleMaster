Welcome to WiffleMaster. WiffleMaster is a webapp for managing teams, players, and leagues, and keeping score of wiffleball games. As a user, you can create players, teams, and leagues. You can add players to teams and add teams to leagues. You can then keep score of games between these teams, taking place as part of a league or independently. You can view players' and team's statistics and make managerial decisions accordingly.

Please see rules and explanations for more information on how wiffleball is played and what the different statistics mean.
